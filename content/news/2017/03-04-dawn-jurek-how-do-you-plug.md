<p><span style="color: #000000;">Did you know that you can amplify your ServiceNow instance with plugins? Or maybe you already know about them, but aren't sure how to "plug them in"? <span style="font-family: arial, sans-serif;">In this ninth installment of our best practices series, we offer plugins best practices to help you navigate the following scenarios:</span></span></p><p></p><ul><li><span style="color: #000000;">You may already have the desired plugin activated on your instance, but you don't know how to find out.</span></li><li><span style="color: #000000;">You want to activate a plugin, but you can't find it in the <strong>System Definition &gt; Plugins</strong> module.</span></li><li><span style="color: #000000;">When requesting a plugin through HI, the form requires you to set the planned date and time at least two business days in the future. But you need the plugin before that!</span></li></ul><p></p><p><span style="color: #000000;">Before we delve into the details, here's a quick primer on plugins.</span></p><p></p><h2><span style="color: #000000;">What's a plugin?</span></h2><p><span style="color: #000000;">Plugins are software components that provide specific features and enhanced functionalities within a ServiceNow Enterprise instance. New plugins become available with each new release. Check out the </span><a title="ocs.servicenow.com/bundle/istanbul-servicenow-platform/page/administer/plugins/reference/istanbul-list-of-plugins.html" href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/istanbul-servicenow-platform/page/administer/plugins/reference/istanbul-list-of-plugins.html">list of plugins available on the Istanbul release</a>.</p><p></p><p><span style="color: #000000;">Certain plugins are activated by default on a new Enterprise instance. When you upgrade, most plugins aren't enabled on your upgraded Enterprise instance. If you want additional plugins, you must activate them. Users with the admin role can activate some plugins through self-service. Other plugins must be requested through HI and activated by ServiceNow personnel, either because they require a subscription or for some other reasonâ€”for example, because they are appropriate only for certain deployments. Plugins cannot be deactivated once they are activated, with a few exceptions.</span></p><p></p><h2><span style="color: #000000;">Plugins best practices:</span></h2><p><span style="color: #000000;">When activating a plugin on your ServiceNow instance, follow these best practices. (Role required: admin):</span></p><p></p><h2 style="padding-left: 30px;"><span style="color: #000000;">Avoid mistaking one plugin for another</span></h2><ul><ul><li><span style="color: #000000;">Write down the name of the plugin you want. Some plugins have similar names that can be mistaken for each other.</span></li></ul></ul><p></p><h2 style="padding-left: 30px;"><span style="color: #000000;">Check to see if the plugin is already active on your instance</span></h2><ul><ul><li><span style="color: #000000;">You can do this by entering <strong>sys_plugins.list </strong>in the navigation filter and searching for the plugin. This list includes all plugins that are active on the instance, whether they were activated by default when the instance was new or upgraded, through self-service, or by ServiceNow personnel at your request:</span></li></ul></ul><p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong><img  __jive_id="248699" alt="Plugins--sys_plugins.list2.jpg" class="image-1 jive-image" src="bd4d850adb14d344e9737a9e0f961905.iix" style="width: 620px; height: 419px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"/></strong></span></p><ul><ul><li><span style="color: #000000;">Don't rely on the <strong>System Definition &gt; Plugins </strong>module to determine which plugins are active on the instance. That module lists only plugins available through self-service.</span></li></ul></ul><p style="padding-left: 30px;"></p><h2 style="padding-left: 30px;"><span style="color: #000000;">Search for a plugin to see if it's available for self-service activation</span></h2><ul><ul><li><span style="color: #000000;">You can figure out if the desired plugin is available for self-service activation, and if so, activate it yourself. Begin by searching for the plugin in the <strong>System Definition &gt; Plugins</strong> module:</span></li></ul></ul><p><span style="text-align: center; color: #000000;"><img  __jive_id="248724" alt="Plugins--Plugins-list2.jpg" class="image-2 jive-image" src="0c4ae80adb94dfc03eb27a9e0f9619cb.iix" style="width: 620px; height: 419px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"/></span></p><ul><ul><li><span style="color: #000000;">If it's not listed there, it isn't available for self-service activation.</span></li><li><span style="color: #000000;">If you find the plugin in the list, follow the procedure to</span> <a title="ocs.servicenow.com/bundle/istanbul-servicenow-platform/page/administer/plugins/task/t_ActivateAPlugin.html" href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/istanbul-servicenow-platform/page/administer/plugins/task/t_ActivateAPlugin.html">activate a plugin.</a></li></ul></ul><p style="padding-left: 30px;"></p><h2 style="padding-left: 30px;"><span style="color: #000000;">Request a plugin through HI</span></h2><ul><ul><li><span style="color: #000000;">If the desired plugin isn't listed in the <strong>System Definition &gt; Plugins</strong> module,</span> <a title="ocs.servicenow.com/bundle/istanbul-servicenow-platform/page/administer/plugins/task/t_RequestAPlugin.html" href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/istanbul-servicenow-platform/page/administer/plugins/task/t_RequestAPlugin.html">request the plugin through HI</a> <span style="color: #000000;">by navigating to <strong>Service Catalog &gt; Activate Plugin</strong>:</span></li></ul></ul><p><span style="text-align: center; color: #000000;"><img  __jive_id="248735" alt="Plugins--HI-service-catalog-form.jpg" class="image-3 jive-image" src="c978c486db9c5fc03eb27a9e0f9619c4.iix" style="width: 620px; height: 405px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"/></span></p><ol start="4"><ul><li><span style="color: #000000;">On the request form, you must select a time at least 48 hours in the future. However, if you want the plugin activated before 48 hours, specify the time and date you need it in the <strong>Reason/Comments</strong> field before submitting the form. Even after submitting the form, you can request the plugin sooner by opening the change request and adding a comment to specify the date and time you need it.</span></li></ul></ol><p><span style="text-align: center; color: #000000;"><img  __jive_id="248737" alt="Plugins--HI-plugin-request-form.jpg" class="image-4 jive-image" src="31e363f1db541fc03eb27a9e0f961961.iix" style="width: 620px; height: 509px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"/></span></p><p style="padding-left: 30px;"></p><h2 style="padding-left: 30px;"><span style="color: #000000;">Still have questions? Get more information about ServiceNow plugins</span></h2><p style="padding-left: 30px;"><span style="padding-left: 30px; color: #000000;">For more information about ServiceNow plugins, see:</span></p><ul><ul><ul><li><a title="ocs.servicenow.com/bundle/istanbul-servicenow-platform/page/administer/plugins/task/t_ActivateAPlugin.html" href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/istanbul-servicenow-platform/page/administer/plugins/task/t_ActivateAPlugin.html">Activate a plugin</a></li><li><a title="ocs.servicenow.com/bundle/istanbul-servicenow-platform/page/administer/plugins/task/t_RequestAPlugin.html#t_RequestAPlugin" href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/istanbul-servicenow-platform/page/administer/plugins/task/t_RequestAPlugin.html#t_RequestAPlugin">Request a plugin</a></li><li><a title="ocs.servicenow.com/bundle/istanbul-servicenow-platform/page/administer/plugins/task/t_PurchaseAPlugin.html" href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/istanbul-servicenow-platform/page/administer/plugins/task/t_PurchaseAPlugin.html">Purchase a plugin</a></li></ul></ul></ul><p style="font-weight: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-family: inherit; color: #666666;"></p><p style="font-weight: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-family: inherit; color: #666666;"><span style="color: #000000;">--</span></p><p></p><p style="font-weight: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-family: inherit;"><span style="color: #000000;">Behind the scenes here at ServiceNow, the Knowledge Management and Multimedia teams work closely with subject matter experts to disseminate critical information to our customers.
