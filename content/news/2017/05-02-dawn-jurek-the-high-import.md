<p>Employees get sick. Their children get sick. Employees' children's pets get sick. They take vacations—not the pets, but the employees. They leave the company—sometimes without notice. Your organization probably has a comprehensive set of contingency plans for these inevitable events, but does your plan include managing your ServiceNow company contacts to ensure that important notifications still get through?</p><p></p><p>In this 16th installment of our <a title="" _jive_internal="true" href="/content?filterID=all~objecttype~objecttype%5Bblogpost%5D&amp;filterID=all~tag%5Bnowsupport+best+practices+series%5D&amp;query=nowsupport+best+practices">best practice series</a>, we look at why you should keep your company contacts up to date, and how to do it.</p><p style="font-family: arial, sans-serif; color: #666666;"><span style="font-style: inherit; font-family: inherit;"><strong>__________________</strong></span></p><p></p><h2>Why should you keep your company contacts up to date?</h2><p>As a ServiceNow customer, we want to keep you abreast of everything you need to know about your instances and any issues that arise. To that end, you can manage your company contacts on HI anytime. <span style="color: #666666; font-family: arial, sans-serif;">Partner administrators can manage users in their own company and users in companies with which the partner is associated.</span></p><p></p><p>Here's a list of company contacts you designate in HI, and the type of notifications each one gets:</p><p></p><table border="1" class="jiveBorder" height="308" style="border: 1px solid #c6c6c6; width: 797px; height: 299px;"><tbody><tr><th style="text-align: left; background-color: #f2f2f2; color: #505050; padding: 6px;" valign="middle"><strong>Company Contact<br/></strong></th><th style="text-align: left; background-color: #f2f2f2; color: #505050; padding: 6px;" valign="middle"><strong>Notifications<br/></strong></th></tr><tr><td style="padding: 6px;"><p>Primary Support Contact/</p><p>Secondary Support Contact</p></td><td style="padding: 6px;"><p>Mass outage or maintenance window</p><p>New incident for the company (if Caller field is empty)</p><p>New clone request</p></td></tr><tr><td style="padding: 6px;"><p>Primary Technical Contact/</p><p>Secondary Technical Contact</p></td><td style="padding: 6px;"><p>Mass outage or maintenance window</p><p>New incident for the company (if Caller field is empty)</p><p>New instance is provisioned</p></td></tr><tr><td style="padding: 6px;"><p>Users in the Communication list</p></td><td style="padding: 6px;"><p>Patches, auto-upgrades, and upgrades</p></td></tr><tr><td style="padding: 6px;">Users in the Notification list</td><td style="padding: 6px;"><p>Auto-upgrades, upgrades, all incidents opened and incidents updated with comments</p></td></tr><tr><td style="padding: 6px;">Customer Administrator</td><td style="padding: 6px;"><p>Dormant user report</p><p>Customer administrator roles requests</p></td></tr><tr><td style="padding: 6px;">Security Contact</td><td style="padding: 6px;">Review, discuss and authorize security-related issues with ServiceNow's security team</td></tr></tbody></table><p></p><h2>How to update your company contacts on HI</h2><p><span style="color: #666666; font-family: arial, sans-serif;">You must have the </span><span style="font-family: arial, sans-serif; color: #666666;"><strong>customer_admin</strong></span><span style="color: #666666; font-family: arial, sans-serif;"> or <strong>partner_admin</strong> role (</span><span style="font-family: arial, sans-serif; color: #666666;"><strong>business_customer_admin</strong></span><span style="color: #666666; font-family: arial, sans-serif;"> in Express) to update company contacts on HI. </span></p><p style="padding-left: 30px;">1. Navigate to <a title="i.service-now.com/hisp" href="https://hi.service-now.com/hisp">https://hi.service-now.com/hisp</a> and log in.</p><p style="padding-left: 30px;">2. Click <strong>Manage Accounts</strong>.</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img  alt="manage_accounts_link.jpg" class="image-6 jive-image" src="eccfffbddb18d3049c9ffb651f961904.iix" style="width: 620px; height: 341px;"/></p><p style="padding-left: 30px;">3. The Manage Accounts page appears. If you're a partner administrator, you can manage users in partner companies by clicking your name in the header and selecting <strong>Switch Company.</strong></p><p style="padding-left: 30px; text-align: center;"><img  alt="manage_contacts_switch_company copy.jpg" class="image-7 jive-image" src="6cff9846dbdc9fc03eb27a9e0f96198f.iix" style="width: 620px; height: 89px;"/></p><p style="padding-left: 30px;"></p><p style="padding-left: 30px;">4. Scroll down to the <strong>All Contacts</strong> section.</p><p style="padding-left: 30px;"></p><p style="padding-left: 30px;"><img   alt="manage_contacts_accounts_page.jpg" class="image-4 jive-image" src="91f694c6db189fc03eb27a9e0f961903.iix" style="width: 620px; height: 352px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"/></p><p style="padding-left: 30px;"></p><p style="padding-left: 30px;">5. Click each tab to update the contacts. To update a contact, click the <strong>Name</strong> field and search for and select the user. The remaining fields for that user will be automatically populated.</p><p style="padding-left: 30px;"><img   alt="manage_contacts_select_contact.jpg" class="image-2 jive-image" src="0e51ed42dbdc1b04ed6af3231f961923.iix" style="width: 620px; height: 178px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"/>6. To edit the Communication or Notification list, click the <strong>Communication &amp; Notification</strong> section.</p><p style="padding-left: 30px;">7. Click the name(s) in the field if the field is already populated, or click the empty field. Select the user names and click <strong>Save</strong>. Click <strong>Close</strong>.</p><p><img   alt="manage_contacts_commslist.jpg" class="image-3 jive-image" src="ed8bb0cadb1c9344e9737a9e0f961951.iix" style="width: 620px; height: 181px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"/></p><p></p><p>For more information, check out these knowledge base articles:</p><ul><li><a title="i.service-now.com/kb_view.do?sysparm_article=KB0547262" href="https://hi.service-now.com/kb_view.do?sysparm_article=KB0547262">Managing company contacts on HI</a></li><li><a title="i.service-now.com/kb_view.do?sysparm_article=KB0547254" href="https://hi.service-now.com/kb_view.do?sysparm_article=KB0547254">Notifications on HI</a></li><li><a title="i.service-now.com/kb_view.do?sysparm_article=KB0522465" href="https://hi.service-now.com/kb_view.do?sysparm_article=KB0522465">Partner administration | process and procedure</a></li></ul><p style="font-weight: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-family: inherit;">--</p><p style="font-weight: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-family: inherit;"></p><p style="font-weight: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-family: inherit;">Behind the scenes here at ServiceNow, the Knowledge Management and Multimedia teams work closely with subject matter experts to disseminate critical information to our customers.
