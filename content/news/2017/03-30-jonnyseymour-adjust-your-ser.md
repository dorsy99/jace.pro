<p><a title="n.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daylight_saving_time" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daylight_saving_time">Daylight Saving Time (DST)</a> is the practice of advancing clocks during summer months by one hour so that evening daylight lasts an hour longer, while sacrificing normal sunrise times. Typically, regions that use Daylight Saving Time adjust clocks forward one hour close to the start of spring and adjust them backward in the autumn to standard time.</p><p></p><p>When the DST is enabled on some <a title="n.wikipedia.org/wiki/Time_zone" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Time_zone">time zones (TZ)</a>, clients and applications will adjust the timezone offset twice a year. Note that only the "Display" value is changing. The time is stored in UTC and is not affected by the display change.</p><p></p><h3>The key for a successful DTS change is to have both client and apps update the offset at the same time.</h3><p></p><p>If a <a title="ocs.servicenow.com/bundle/istanbul-servicenow-platform/page/administer/time/reference/r_TimeZones.html" href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/istanbul-servicenow-platform/page/administer/time/reference/r_TimeZones.html">time zone in a ServiceNow instance</a> is specified based on location (for example, America/Los Angeles), times will automatically be adjusted for Daylight Saving Time. If a time zone is specified based on the name of a time zone (for example, GMT), it will not adjust.</p><p></p><p><img   alt="getready-for-daytimesaving.jpeg" class="image-1 jive-image" height="146" src="305ccd42db14d344e9737a9e0f9619c6.iix" style="width: 260px; height: 145.948px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="260"/></p><h1>Adjusting your ServiceNow instance time zone after Daylight Saving Time</h1><p>I have a few recommendations for what to do after a DTS change to <a title="ocs.servicenow.com/bundle/istanbul-servicenow-platform/page/administer/time/task/t_SetASystemTimeZone.html" href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/istanbul-servicenow-platform/page/administer/time/task/t_SetASystemTimeZone.html">set your time zone</a>.
