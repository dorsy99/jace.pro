<p>Now that Istanbul has dropped there are a number of blogs out there introducing some of the new features, but I wanted to call out the top features for technology partners looking to submit or update their apps in the <a title="tore.servicenow.com/" href="https://store.servicenow.com/">ServiceNow Store</a>. The following are the top 5 features of Istanbul that I think will be most impactful to developers submitting applications to the store including things that make developing on ServiceNow easier and also add value to your applications.</p><p></p><p><strong>1. Automated Test Framework</strong><br/>Istanbul includes an initial release of the automated testing framework for ServiceNow. With this framework you can script some test cases, and include them in your application so your customers can automate their testing when upgrading.
