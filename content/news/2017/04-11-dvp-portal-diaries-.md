<p style="margin-bottom: .0001pt;">In this article, I would like to discuss on how to display multiple catalogs in the service portal. We already know that OOB Service Portal supports only a single catalog, which is not a desired solution most of the clients are looking for. In order to support multiple catalogs, below are a couple solutions that I read.</p><p></p><ol style="list-style-type: decimal;"><li>Create a separate portal for each catalog or</li><li>Use the solution proposed by @Brett Karl which consolidates all the categories.</li></ol><p></p><p style="margin-bottom: .0001pt;">This is a solution that I came up with.
