<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">Allan has a conversation with Boaz Hecht of SkyGiraffe about building next generation enterprise mobile applications, keeping up in the fast-paced world of mobile innovation, and what to expect from ServiceNow mobile applications in the future.</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">With <a title="ww.businesswire.com/news/home/20171025005601/en/" href="https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20171025005601/en/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">SkyGiraffe</a>, ServiceNow packaged apps, including IT Service Management, will be delivered in a native mobile format. Customers will also be able to deliver consumer-like mobile experiences for any application built on the Now Platform. Because the mobile experience will be native, ServiceNow will also support offline applications.</span></p>
