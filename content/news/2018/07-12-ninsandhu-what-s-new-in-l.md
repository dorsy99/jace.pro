<p><span style="font-size: 14.0pt;">In the London release for HR Service Delivery, it&#39;s all about making it even easier for employees to get service, while improving HR efficiency. We have introduced new</span><span style="font-size: 14pt;"> features like <strong>Employee Document Management</strong>, <strong>Virtual Agent for HR Service Delivery</strong>, <strong>Targeted Content Automation</strong>, and <strong>Knowledge Blocks</strong>,<strong> </strong>which are specifically designed to enhance the employee service experience.</span></p>
<h1><span style="; color: #7030a0; font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Employee Document Management</strong></span></h1>
<h4><strong>Electronically manage employee documents from hire to retire</strong></h4>
<p><span style="font-size: 18.6667px;">As one of our most requested capabilities, we are excited to unveil our new Employee Document Management application in this release, which is designed to help HR manage employee documents more efficiently, securely, and ensure compliance. By going paperless, HR can now easily set document retention rules based on corporate and legal regulations, set document permissions and control access, and define metadata tags on documents for easy search and retrieval.</span></p>

<h1><span style="color: #7030a0; font-size: 14.0pt;"><strong>Virtual Agent for HR Service Delivery</strong></span></h1>
<h4><strong>Take advantage of automation and improve employee self-service</strong></h4>
<p><span style="font-size: 14.0pt;">We have now made it even easier for employees to quickly get answers or complete common transactions though the release of our automated conversational chatbot. Our new Virtual Agent chatbot for HR delivers out-of-the-box conversational service experiences and automates interactions for everything from pay discrepancies to updating a employee profile.</span></p>
<p> <img style="max-width: 100%; max-height: 480px;" src="c5995cf4db571b4067a72926ca96192b.iix" /></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Make it even easier for employees to get answers to questions.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14.0pt;"><br /><span style="color: #7030a0;"><strong>Targeted Content Automation</strong></span></span></p>
<h4><strong>Proactively distribute targeted, scheduled communications to employees</strong></h4>
<p><span style="font-size: 14.0pt;">Employee communications just got more personalized in this release with our new Targeted Content Automation feature. Proactively distribute targeted and scheduled communications to employees, making it easier for employees to get access to the information they need. HR can now modernize and streamline how they send communications around open enrollment, new hire onboarding, local events, and other important communications by: </span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><span style="font-size: 14.0pt;">• Delivering employees the right information at the right moment </span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><span style="font-size: 14.0pt;">• Communicating announcements, events, or initiatives via e-mail or Employee  Service Center </span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><span style="font-size: 14.0pt;">• Targeting specific employee audiences using dynamic system or user data </span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><span style="font-size: 14.0pt;">• Scheduling communications based on fixed dates </span></p>

<h1><span style="font-size: 14.0pt;"><span style="color: #7030a0;"><strong>Knowledge Blocks</strong></span></span></h1>
<h4>Simplify knowledge base article management and display only user-relevant article content </h4>
<p><span style="font-size: 14.0pt;">As anyone in HR knows, managing multiple knowledge articles that contain specific information for different employee segments has always been a time consuming-process for HR. With the introduction of our new Knowledge Blocks feature, that&#39;s all about to change! HR can now create a single universal knowledge article which leverages re-usable blocks of content that can be secured by user criteria. Now, employees can reference one master knowledge article and only see sections of content that are relevant to them. Making it #EasyForEmployees to find the information they need, while improving HR efficiency.</span></p>
