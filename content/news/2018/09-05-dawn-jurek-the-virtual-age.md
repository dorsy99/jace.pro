<p>Have you ever called a customer service number to get an answer to a question or make a request, only to be bounced from one agent to another like the ball in a pinball machine? It&#39;s just as bad on the other side. Routine IT, HR and customer service requests can bog down agents and impact your entire service operation. </p>

<p style="text-align: left;">In this installment of our <a title="" href="community?id&#61;community_blog&amp;sys_id&#61;6e7d6269dbd0dbc01dcaf3231f9619c0" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">NOWSupport best practices series</a>, we look at the ServiceNow Virtual Agent chatbot solution, new in the London release, and give you resources to learn more.  </p>
<h3>What is Virtual Agent?</h3>
<p>Virtual Agent is a versatile chatbot that can:</p>
<ul><li>Improve service via 24/7 automated support</li><li>Reduce agent workload</li><li>Scale business efficiency</li><li>Switch to a live agent when needed</li></ul>
<p>Virtual Agent can walk users through complex tasks, giving guidance along the way, and handing off to a live agent if the user needs a little extra attention. And it can give your system an engaging personality to enhance your users&#39; experience.</p>

<h3>How do I get Virtual Agent? </h3>
<p>Virtual Agent is provided with the following packages:</p>
<ul><li>ITSM Professional</li><li>Customer Service Management Professional </li><li>HR Service Delivery Professional</li><li>HR Service Delivery Enterprise</li></ul>
<p>Request the Virtual Agent plugin (com.glide.cs.chatbot) on HI for your non-production instance. The plugin must be activated by ServiceNow personnel.</p>
<p>Next, <a title="implement Virtual Agent" href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/london-servicenow-platform/page/administer/virtual-agent/task/Implement-virtual-agent.html" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">implement Virtual Agent</a>, then develop and test your Virtual Agent conversations before deploying to a production instance. Virtual Agent comes with prebuilt bot conversations for each business area to help users with common self-service tasks. You can modify these topics, or build your own.</p>
