<p><span style="font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;"><em>Posted by </em></span><a href="/community?id=community_user_profile&user=83a3d62ddb1c1fc09c9ffb651f96192e" rel="nofollow"><span style="color: blue; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><em>Liam Yu</em></span></a><em> <span style="font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;">January 11, 2018</span></em></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif; color: #666666;">In the Kingston release, we are very excited about introducing greater user experience innovation in IT Operations Management as well as further process automation with the Now Platform<sup>TM</sup>. With Kingston, we are helping you to eliminate service outages and speed up operations across your growing IT environments better than ever before. Let&#39;s take look at what&#39;s new...</span></p>
<p> </p>
<hr />
<p style="margin-left: 5.0pt;"> </p>
<p style="margin-left: 5.0pt;"><span style="font-size: 16.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri Light&#39;,sans-serif; color: #1f497d;">Service Mapping: Traffic-based Discovery</span><span style="color: #1f497d; font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;"><strong><br /> <br /> </strong></span></p>
<p style="margin-left: 5.0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;"><strong>New Capability</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin-left: 5.0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;">Mapping services across your IT environments can sometimes face delays when your needs extend across multiple networks and subnets. This can not only slow down your initial mapping but also impact ongoing discovery efforts as your organization changes and grows. We have added advanced traffic-based discovery that will accelerate the service mapping process in two ways. First, we can use traffic-based discovery to simultaneously identify dozens or hundreds of service mapping candidates based upon network traffic. </span></p>
<p> </p>
<p style="margin-left: 5.0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;"><strong>Customer Value</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin-left: 5.0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;">First, this simplifies the process of getting started. Second, we can map out the business services using network information as the basis for the map. This allows a functional business service map to be generated quickly by the system and then refined over time by your subject matter experts.   This new traffic-based discovery capability greatly extends the ability to identify and create initial maps for business services and speeds up the ongoing discovery process with less effort and better accuracy.</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p style="margin-left: 5.0pt;"><span style="font-size: 16.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri Light&#39;,sans-serif; color: #1f497d;">Service Mapping: Guided User Experience and Process Automation</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p style="margin-left: 5.0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;"><strong><img class="jive-image image-10" style="max-width: 1200px; max-height: 900px; width: 346px; height: 195.49px; float: right;" src="7217e7f1db50df04e9737a9e0f961944.iix" width="346" height="195" /></strong></span></p>
<p style="margin-left: 5.0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;"><strong>New Capability</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin-left: 5.0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;">We&#39;ve upgraded the user interface with added process automation to enable hundreds of business services to be mapped simultaneously. Many customers have asked for guidance through the mapping process to help speed up and simplify the effort for faster time-to-service visibility. This new interface allows easier adoption, faster time to map services, and better usability of service maps. The new user interface guides the user by grouping and organizing issues for faster time-to-visibility resolution.   The screenshot (Figure 1) shows the easy-to-use landing page for the new Service Mapping interface. You can see four boxes that show the status of the mapping process including <strong>Map, Fix, Approve,</strong> and <strong>Completed</strong>.   A simple click allows the user to drill down and take action.</span></p>
<p style="margin-left: 5.0pt;"> </p>
<p style="margin-left: 5.0pt;"><span style="font-family: Calibri, sans-serif; font-size: 14.6667px; color: #505050;">Figure 1</span></p>
<p style="margin-left: 5.0pt;"> </p>
<p style="margin-left: 5.0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;"><strong>Customer Value</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin-left: 5.0pt;"><span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;">This enhancement enables IT operations administrators to map services quickly and speed the time it takes to gain visibility of how their infrastructure enables their business services.</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri Light&#39;,sans-serif; color: #1f497d;">Event Management: New Performance Analytics KPIs</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;"><img class="image-9 jive-image" style="max-width: 1200px; max-height: 900px; width: 335px; height: 175.188px; float: right;" src="ca66c44adb5c5fc068c1fb651f961999.iix" width="335" height="175" /></span><span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;"><strong>New Capability</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;">Defining key performance indicators (KPIs) can be difficult without guidance and a broad set of options to choose from. A series of new KPIs and Performance Analytics reports have been added to enable customers to gain insight into their alert activity and response effectiveness. IT operations managers can better understand how events and alerts are being managed over time.<br /> </span></p>
<p><span style="color: #505050; font-size: 14.6667px; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;">Figure 2</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;"><strong>Customer Value</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;">They can quickly measure improvements, set benchmark targets, and gain insights into their alert activity to help with continuous improvement efforts. The screenshot (Figure 2) shows how easy and customizable it is to view events and manage alerts. Note: For a full list, please review the Event Management release notes and product documentation.</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri Light&#39;,sans-serif; color: #1f497d;">Application Content Import</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #7f7f7f; font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;"><em>For Service Mapping, Discovery and Cloud Management</em></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;"><strong>New Capability</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;">Customers asked for greater flexibility to add application content at their own pace without having to wait for a new product release. We have therefore added a new import capability for content that allows customers to add application content independent of ServiceNow releases. This allows the addition of Discovery and Service Mapping patterns when a customer needs them, as well as the addition of cloud integrations for Cloud Management.</span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;"><strong>Customer Value</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: &#39;Calibri&#39;,sans-serif;">What this means is that IT operations managers now have the flexibility to detect different types of devices, applications, and services in their environments (including within cloud providers, such as AWS and Microsoft Azure) however and whenever they choose. Leveraging new content as it becomes available, instead of waiting for a future release and upgrade to that release, helps provide greater and more immediate value to our customers.</span></p>
