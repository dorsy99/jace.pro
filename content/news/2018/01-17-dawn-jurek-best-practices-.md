This makes it possible to consolidate fragmented tools and legacy systems, while automating processes.</em></p><p align="right" style="text-align: right;">—<a title="ww.servicenow.com/content/dam/servicenow/documents/datasheets/ds-itsm.pdf" href="https://www.servicenow.com/content/dam/servicenow/documents/datasheets/ds-itsm.pdf">ServiceNow IT Service Management Data Sheet</a></p><p>-----</p><p></p><p>In this article of our <span style="font-family: arial, sans-serif; color: #666666;"> </span><a title="" _jive_internal="true" data-containerid="2927" data-containertype="37" data-objectid="7090" data-objecttype="38" href="/community?id=community_blog&sys_id=6e7d6269dbd0dbc01dcaf3231f9619c0" style="font-family: arial, sans-serif; color: #3778c7;">NOWSupport best practices series</a><span style="color: #666666; font-family: arial, sans-serif;">, </span>we offer some suggestions for successfully implementing and maintaining a simple, industry-standard approach to IT service management in your organization. First, check out the <a title="outu.be/D86inB2T-go" href="https://youtu.be/D86inB2T-go">ITSM Overview | Tips for a Successful Implementation</a> video on our <a title="ww.youtube.com/user/servicenowdemo" href="https://www.youtube.com/user/servicenowdemo">NOWsupport YouTube channel</a>:</p><p></p><p style="text-align: center;"><iframe frameborder="0" height="360" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/D86inB2T-go?rel=0" width="640"> 
</iframe></p><p>-----</p><p>Now, here are four tips for implementing and long-term success in maintaining IT service management in your ServiceNow instance:</p><p></p><h2>Become familiar with the ITIL framework</h2><p>The IT Infrastructure Library, <a title="ocs.servicenow.com/bundle/kingston-servicenow-platform/page/product/configuration-management/concept/c_ITIL.html" href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/kingston-servicenow-platform/page/product/configuration-management/concept/c_ITIL.html">ITIL</a>, is an integrated, process-based best practice methodology for managing IT Services. Its focus is to optimize IT service delivery and support to improve business operations. The ITIL framework consists of five stages: Service Strategy, Service Design, Service Transition, Service Operation, and Continual Service Improvement. In each of these areas, you will build a process to address user requests, maintenance, service restoration, and performance improvement. ServiceNow ITSM applications and processes align with the ITIL methodology, so familiarity with the ITIL framework will help you understand how ITSM workflow and automation work in the ServiceNow platform.</p><p><img   alt="ITIL_Framework.png" class="image-3 jive-image" src="161727f1db50df04e9737a9e0f9619b5.iix" style="width: 620px; height: 380px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"/></p><p></p><h2>Implement a sound governance model</h2><p></p><p>Governance provides a structure for sound business decisions. It helps key personnel understand business requirements, develop the ServiceNow platform, and run projects in an organized way. Implementing a sound governance model—including a platform support team—allows the ServiceNow platform to scale and mature along with your business needs.</p><p>Here are some resources for getting started on building a platform support team:</p><ul><li><span style="color: #9900ff;"><a title="Building the ultimate ServiceNow platform team" __default_attr="5684" __jive_macro_name="blogpost" class="jive_macro jive_macro_blogpost" data-orig-content="Building the ultimate ServiceNow platform team" data-renderedposition="1340.796875_38_317_16" href="/community?id=community_blog&sys_id=c3bc6e25dbd0dbc01dcaf3231f961930">Building the ultimate ServiceNow platform team</a></span></li><li><span style="color: #9900ff;"><a href="https://hi.service-now.com/kb_view.do?sysparm_article=KB0553714" title="https://hi.service-now.com/kb_view.do?sysparm_article=KB0553714">ServiceNow KB: Platform Support Model (KB0553714)</a> </span></li></ul><p></p><h2>Use configurations instead of custom mode</h2><p></p><p>Customizations require time and effort to create and maintain, may result in performance degradation, and complicate upgrades—given the level of testing required. Instead, take advantage of the configurations built into the platform. The <a title="" _jive_internal="true" href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/kingston-it-service-management/page/product/itsm_guided_setup/concept/c_ITSMGuidedSetup.html">ITSM Guided Setup</a> is a great place to start and helps you to set up all of your basic ITSM applications, including <a title="ocs.servicenow.com/bundle/kingston-it-service-management/page/product/incident-management/concept/c_IncidentManagement.html" href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/kingston-it-service-management/page/product/incident-management/concept/c_IncidentManagement.html">Incident</a>, <a title="ocs.servicenow.com/bundle/kingston-it-service-management/page/product/problem-management/concept/c_ProblemManagement.html" href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/kingston-it-service-management/page/product/problem-management/concept/c_ProblemManagement.html">Problem</a>, and <a title="ocs.servicenow.com/bundle/kingston-it-service-management/page/product/change-management/concept/c_ITILChangeManagement.html" href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/kingston-it-service-management/page/product/change-management/concept/c_ITILChangeManagement.html">Change</a> Management. When you're ready, it will also guide you through configuring the <a title="ocs.servicenow.com/bundle/kingston-servicenow-platform/page/product/configuration-management/concept/c_ConfigurationManagementDatabase.html" href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/kingston-servicenow-platform/page/product/configuration-management/concept/c_ConfigurationManagementDatabase.html">Configuration Management Database</a> (CMDB), <a title="ocs.servicenow.com/bundle/kingston-servicenow-platform/page/product/knowledge-management/topic/p_KnowledgeManagment.html" href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/kingston-servicenow-platform/page/product/knowledge-management/topic/p_KnowledgeManagment.html">Knowledge Base</a>, <a title="ocs.servicenow.com/bundle/kingston-it-service-management/page/product/service-catalog-management/concept/c_ServiceCatalogManagement.html" href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/kingston-it-service-management/page/product/service-catalog-management/concept/c_ServiceCatalogManagement.html">Service Catalog</a>, and other advanced ITSM capabilities.</p><p></p><p style="text-align: center;"><img   alt="ITSM_Guided_Setup.png" class="image-2 jive-image" src="e5668c8edb1c5fc03eb27a9e0f96191f.iix" style="width: 620px; height: 394px;"/></p><p></p><h2>Upgrade to new releases</h2><p></p><p>Each release includes security and bug fixes, new applications and features, and enhancements to existing functionality. Upgrading ensures platform security and allows you to take advantage of new features. If you aren't using many customizations, adopting new releases should be easy.</p><p></p><p>-----</p><p></p><h2>For more information, see:</h2><ul><li><a title="ocs.servicenow.com/bundle/kingston-it-service-management/page/product/it-service-management/reference/r_ITServiceManagement.html" href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/kingston-it-service-management/page/product/it-service-management/reference/r_ITServiceManagement.html">IT Service Management</a> (product documentation)</li><li><a title="ocs.servicenow.com/bundle/kingston-it-service-management/page/product/itsm_guided_setup/concept/c_ITSMGuidedSetup.html" href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/kingston-it-service-management/page/product/itsm_guided_setup/concept/c_ITSMGuidedSetup.html">ITSM guided setup</a> (product documentation)</li><li><a title="i.service-now.com/kb_view.do?sysparm_article=KB0621384" href="https://hi.service-now.com/kb_view.do?sysparm_article=KB0621384">ITSM Process Guides</a> (KB article;
