<h1>IBM and VMware Publisher Packs</h1>
<p><span style="font-size: 11.0pt;">Software Audits continue to be one of the largest pain points we hear from customers. The IBM pack will allow you to integrate with IBM&#39;s ILMT to gather PVU and RVU consumption information so that an accurate license position can be produced and evaluated alongside the rest of your software portfolio. The VMware pack features Per Processor and Per Application Instance license metrics to license products such as vCenter or vSphere. Using ServiceNow Discovery and the VMware publisher pack together enables a full vCenter topology view and your compliance position.</span></p>

<h1>Office 365 Subscription Management</h1>
<p><span style="font-size: 11.0pt;">Office 365 Subscription Management provides customers the ability to manage cloud software subscription consumption and the corresponding on premise installations all from a single place. You can now monitor consumption levels to contain costs.   You can also leverage Performance Analytics to forecast future subscription needs based on past trends for your renewal negotiations. </span></p>

<h1 style="text-align: left;">Reconciliation Dimensions</h1>
<p><span style="font-size: 11.0pt;">Many of our customers organize their business into component units such as subsidiaries, departments or geographies. <span style="font-size: 11.0pt;">Reconciliation grouping dimensions provides a way to track compliance and costs in the same way that you see your business. It facilitates cost allocation and consumption based show backs.   </span></span></p>

<h1> </h1>
<h1>Custom License Metrics</h1>
<p><span style="font-size: 11.0pt;">Custom License Metrics give the capability to create non-typical license metrics which can be used throughout the Software Asset Management product in the same way that other license metrics are used. This means that excess capacity, non-compliance, true-ups and remediation actions can be calculated <span style="text-decoration: underline;"><em>for any software title</em></span>. Any industry specific software you have can be tracked in Software Asset Management.</span></p>

<h1>Content Improvements</h1>
<p><span style="font-size: 11.0pt;">Initially introduced in the Jakarta release, the Content Library provides you access to critical content such as a product repository and normalization rules. Our commitment to growing this Content Library is exemplified by the hundreds of thousands of new normalization rules added by customers who have opted in to crowd sourcing since the Jakarta release. </span></p>
<p><br /><img class="image-9 jive-image" style="height: auto; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="2e0ccccedbd017049c9ffb651f96195b.iix" alt="Sam-kingston-ServiceNow-5.jpg" /></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-size: 11.0pt;">The amazing aspect of the library is that it is available outside of the typical ServiceNow release cycle, meaning that even if you are on the Jakarta release, you can benefit from all this new content being added every week without needing to upgrade.   More content is now available for the Pre-Defined Suite capability initially made available in Jakarta as well. </span></p>

<h1> </h1>
<h1>Blacklisting Software</h1>
<p><span style="font-size: 11.0pt;">Companies have protocols in place to prevent known malicious software or non-work software from being downloaded or installed on end-user devices. However, these protocols are not always 100% effective.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 11.0pt;">Software Blacklisting in Kingston allows the software managers in your organization to create and maintain a blacklist which is used to identify installed software that was not authorized and executes a revocation process to ensure that the software is uninstalled from the device. This will facilitate your organization&#39;s ability to enforce employee compliance and achieve security objectives.</span></p>

<h1>Performance Analytics Dashboards</h1>
<p><span style="font-size: 11.0pt;">Several new dashboards and KPIs available to use out-of-the-box for monitoring </span><span style="font-size: 11.0pt;">software compliance over time and software spend over time. This increased visibility will help our customers make informed decisions based on real data.   Data driven decision making is important for IT planning and contract renewal discussions with major software publishers. </span></p>
