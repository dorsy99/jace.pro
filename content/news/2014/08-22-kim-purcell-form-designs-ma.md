<p>In Eureka and UI14, you can select the Form Design option to customize your forms. It allows you to quickly personalize forms and modify form views in a single UI!</p><p><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-3361-12893/Screen Shot 2014-08-21 at 12.13.11 PM.JPG"><img  alt="Screen Shot 2014-08-21 at 12.13.11 PM.JPG" class="image-0 jive-image" height="263" src="bc0ca7f9db5893041dcaf3231f961956.iix" style="height: 263px; width: 526px;" width="526"/></a></p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p>Form Design simplifies your design tasks by allowing multiple personalization options in your current form. With the new drag-and-drop feature, you can quickly arrange how and what fields appear on your form. You can also remove fields you don't want with a click of a button, create new field types and labels, add and remove sections, and even change field properties without leaving your form. And if you decide you don't want to make these changes, you can quickly revert back using the new <strong>Undo</strong> button. As an added bonus, you can use the same tool to create new form views or navigate to different types of records and customize these forms, too.</p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-3361-12905/Screen Shot 2014-08-21 at 2.04.23 PM.JPG"><img  alt="Screen Shot 2014-08-21 at 2.04.23 PM.JPG" class="jive-image image-2" height="266" src="0610550edb181b04ed6af3231f9619cd.iix" style="height: 265.582px; width: 586px;" width="586"/></a></p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p>If you've upgraded from a previous version, you'll need to activate the Form Design and UI14 plugins:</p><ol><li>Navigate to <strong>System Definition &gt; Plugins</strong>.</li><li>Go to <strong>UI14.</strong> Right-click on the plugin ID and select <strong>Activate/Upgrade</strong>.</li><li>Go to <strong>NG form designer. </strong>Right-click on the plugin ID and select <strong>Activate/Update.</strong></li></ol><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p>You'll now have the option of personalizing your forms using Form Design.</p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p>For more information, see <a title="k-external-small" class="jive-link-external-small" href="http://wiki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=Form_Design" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Form Design</a>.</p>
