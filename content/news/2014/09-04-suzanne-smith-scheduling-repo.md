<p><em>"True genius resides in the capacity for evaluation of uncertain, hazardous, and conflicting information."</em></p><p>Winston Churchill</p><p><span style="color: #000000; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif;"><br/></span></p><p>ServiceNow makes it easy to create a variety of reports that run on a schedule and are distributed through email automatically. Send out regular reports to:</p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><ul><li>show management individual or team progress on important initiatives</li><li>share data with your team</li><li>identify trends that require action</li></ul><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p>You have a great deal of control over when reports are generated and distributed. Use common options such as weekly or monthly, or try some creative options.</p><p><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><br/></span></p><ol><li><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">Open HI.</span></li><li><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">Navigate to <strong>System Definition &gt; Scheduled Jobs</strong>.</span></li><li><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">Click <strong>New</strong>.</span></li><li><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">Click <strong>Automate the generation and distribution of a report</strong>.<br/><br/>The scheduling fun happens on the <strong>Run</strong> field. <br/> <br/><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-3408-13387/schedule_reports.png"><img  alt="schedule_reports.png" class="image-0 jive-image" height="339" src="33f69d06dbd81b04ed6af3231f9619bd.iix" style="width: 620px; height: 317px;" width="664"/></a><br/><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">The option you select in the </span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><strong>Run</strong></span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"> field, hides or shows other options. For example, if you select </span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><strong>Monthly</strong></span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">, a </span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><strong>Day</strong></span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"> and a </span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><strong>Time</strong></span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"> field appear.<br/></span></span></li></ol><p><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><br/></span></p><p><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">Here are some ideas for scheduling reports: <br/></span></p><p><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><br/></span></p><ul><li><span style="text-align: -webkit-auto; color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;">If you select <strong>Periodically</strong> in the </span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;"><strong>Run</strong></span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;"> field, the <strong>Repeat Interval</strong> field appears. Repeat interval is </span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;">the duration between each scheduled report. You can set the duration to days, hours, or minutes. At one extreme, this option gives you the ability to send a report every minute (although, you should really consider if that is necessary!). If you need to run a report every quarter, calculate the number of days in the quarter and enter it in the <strong>Days</strong> field.</span></span></li><li><span style="text-align: -webkit-auto; color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;">To run a report only on Mondays, Tuesdays, and Wednesdays, create 3 separate weekly reports (one for each day).</span></li><li><span style="text-align: -webkit-auto; color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;"><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">For some serious scheduling power, use conditions.
