But then things like public cloud, Web scale, and Heartbleed changed everything, and the practitioners and administrators were left holding an old bag of tools not capable of handling the new job at hand. They need data center and IT automation that is fast, scalable and flexible.</span></p><p></p><p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">It would only make sense to take the best of ITSM and the best of data center automation and configuration management, and put them together to orchestrate all the IT things...from people, process and workflow, to machines, applications, infrastructure, clouds and code.</span></p><p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;"><br/></span></p><p><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">This week at <a title="owledge.servicenow.com/" href="http://knowledge.servicenow.com/" style="color: #e14747;" target="_blank">Knowledge14</a> you can see the best of the best working together. The SaltStack automation engine now has hooks into <a title="w.servicenow.com/" href="http://www.servicenow.com/" style="color: #e14747;" target="_blank">ServiceNow</a> APIs.
