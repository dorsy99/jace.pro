<p><em>"The only reason for time is so that everything doesn't happen at once."</em></p><p>Albert Einstein</p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p>Einstein's statement certainly sounds correct, but if your organization has offices in different time zones, you may feel that everything IS happening at once. Business is taking place 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, and in 24 time zones around the globe. How can you manage people and actions in different locations efficiently? In ServiceNow, there is a lot that you can do with time zones:</p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><ul><li>set the system time zone</li><li>set user time zones</li><li>consider daylight savings time</li><li>try the Time Zone Changer</li><li>do some advanced work in GlideDateTime</li></ul><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p>The <a class="jive-link-external-small" href="http://wiki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=Using_Time_Zones#Setting_System_Time_Zone" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><span style="color: #3366ff; text-decoration: underline;">system time zone</span></a> can be set by navigating to <strong>System Properties &gt; System</strong> and editing the <strong>System timezone, used as default for calendars and users </strong>property. <span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">The new time zone you specify is automatically associated with all users who do not have a time zone identified in their profile.</span></p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p>After a system time zone is set, individual users can <a class="jive-link-external-small" href="http://wiki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=Using_Time_Zones#User_Preferences" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><span style="color: #3366ff; text-decoration: underline;">set a time zone for themselves</span></a> by navigating to <strong>Self-Service &gt; My Profile</strong>. A system administrator can set or change the time zone for any user. It is important to note that <span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">the user is assigned the selected time zone and no longer uses the system time zone</span>. The time zone set on the user record takes precedence over the system time zone.</p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p><a class="jive-link-external-small" href="http://wiki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=Using_Time_Zones#Daylight_Savings_Time" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><span style="color: #3366ff; text-decoration: underline;">Daylight savings time</span></a> can present challenges. To have the system automatically change for daylight savings time, set the time zone based on a location (for example, Europe/Dublin). The time zone does adjust if it is set based on time zone name (for example, GMT).</p><p><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-3322-12422/time_zone_user.png"><img  alt="time_zone_user.png" class="image-1 jive-image" height="452" src="a227044adbdc5704ed6af3231f961925.iix" style="width: 620px; height: 365px;" width="767"/></a></p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p><span style="color: #000000;">In the Harry Potter book named <em>The Prisoner of Azkaban</em>, Hermione Granger uses a nifty device called a Time-Turner to travel through time. Nearly as magical, the ServiceNow</span> <a class="jive-link-external-small" href="http://wiki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=Using_Time_Zones#The_Time_Zone_Changer" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><span style="color: #3366ff; text-decoration: underline;">Time Zone Changer</span></a> <span style="color: #000000;">enables users to </span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><span style="color: #000000;">change their time zone temporarily with a simple drop-down menu. The Time Zone Changer is especially useful for customer support agents helping users in other time zones and for users that travel to different time zones frequently. </span>   </span></p><p><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-3322-12423/time_zone_changer.png"><img  alt="time_zone_changer.png" class="image-0 jive-image" height="76" src="b7fb6b35db9893049c9ffb651f961954.iix" style="height: auto;" width="560"/></a><br/></span></p><p><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><br/></span></p><p style="text-align: -webkit-auto;"><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;">To do some time zone fine-tuning, use the methods in the <a class="jive-link-external-small" href="http://wiki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=GlideDateTime" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><span style="color: #3366ff; text-decoration: underline;">GlideDateTime</span></a> class.
