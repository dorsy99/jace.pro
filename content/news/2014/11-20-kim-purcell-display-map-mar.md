<p>If you are trying to configure your map pages, you may struggle with map markers not showing up on your Google Maps. Map pages on your instance ideally enable you to graphically display data on a Google Map based on the location and link these to your records. These can be easily generated with basic JavaScript; however, your Google Maps API (v.3) may sadly leave you with one lonely marker on your map:</p><p><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-3659-15771/Screen Shot 2014-11-19 at 12.26.13 PM.JPG"><img  alt="Screen Shot 2014-11-19 at 12.26.13 PM.JPG" class="image-0 jive-image" height="299" src="854f9402db10d704ed6af3231f9619df.iix" style="height: 299px; width: 482.125px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="482"/></a></p><p>Google map markers are a type of overlay that are designed to be interactive.
