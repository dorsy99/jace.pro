<p>If you are trying to add a background image to your CMS header, you will likely encounter an issue when you change your Portal Header setting from <strong>Colored Bars</strong> to <strong>Gradient/Image</strong> in versions prior to Eureka:</p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-3645-15607/Screen Shot 2014-11-11 at 11.27.12 AM.JPG"><img  alt="Screen Shot 2014-11-11 at 11.27.12 AM.JPG" class="jive-image image-2" height="214" src="b2eff046db9097049c9ffb651f961935.iix" style="height: 214.269px; width: 335px;" width="335"/></a></p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p>Not only will your background image not show up, but this will also cause your portal page header to go blank. You won't see any of your menu items unless you hover over them (yep, they are still there):</p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-3645-15603/Screen Shot 2014-11-11 at 1.38.14 PM.JPG"><img  alt="Screen Shot 2014-11-11 at 1.38.14 PM.JPG" class="image-0 jive-image" height="255" src="fd7c7331db501344e9737a9e0f9619f1.iix" style="height: 255px; width: 429.62px;" width="430"/></a></p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p>There are a couple of ways to fix this. You can adjust the Default CSS or the UI Macro:</p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><h2><span style="font-size: 12pt;">To fix in Default CSS:</span></h2><ol><li>In the navigation filter, enter <strong>Design</strong>.</li><li>Go to <strong>Style Sheets</strong> &gt; <strong>Default CSS &gt; Gray CSS Header</strong>.</li><li>Add style:   <br/>div.cms_header table.cms_header_table{<br/>         <strong>background: transparent;</strong><br/>}</li></ol><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><h2><span style="font-size: 12pt;">To fix in UI Macro:</span></h2><ol><li>In the navigation filter, enter <strong>UI Macro</strong>.</li><li>Go to<strong> render_content_block_header</strong>.</li><li>Add style:<br/>&lt;table class="cms_header_table" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" border="0" width="100%" <strong>style="background: transparent;</strong>"&gt;</li></ol><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p>You should now be able to enjoy your header and image background on your portal page:</p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-3645-15604/Screen Shot 2014-11-11 at 1.17.02 PM.JPG"><img  alt="Screen Shot 2014-11-11 at 1.17.02 PM.JPG" class="image-1 jive-image" height="265" src="6cf0e14adbd8d304b322f4621f961952.iix" style="height: 264.645px; width: 560px;" width="560"/></a></p><p>For more information, see: <a title="k-external-small" class="jive-link-external-small" href="http://hi.service-now.com/kb_view.do?sysparm_article=KB0533469" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Headers not displaying when background is set to Gradient/Image</a></p>
