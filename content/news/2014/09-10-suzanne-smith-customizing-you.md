<p><em>"Home is where one starts from."</em></p><p>T.S. Eliot</p><p><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><br/></span></p><p><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">To put a twist on T.S. Eliot's words, in ServiceNow you start from your <em>homepage</em>. <br/></span></p><p><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><br/></span></p><p><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">When you log in to <a title="k-external-small" class="jive-link-external-small" href="https://hi.service-now.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">HI</a>, a homepage based on your role appears. For example, an admin often has a different homepage than an itil user. Some users can customize their own homepage. (ESS users most often cannot edit their homepage.) A custom homepage always takes precedence over the default, role-based homepage.</span></p><p><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><br/></span></p><h3><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">How to customize your ServiceNow homepage:<br/></span></h3><p><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><br/></span></p><ol><li><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">To customize your homepage, open the homepage by doing one of the following: <br/><br/></span><ul><li><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">Click the home icon </span><a href="http://wiki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=File:Home.png" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><img alt="Home.png" class="jiveImage" height="16" src="http://wiki.servicenow.com/images/9/9a/Home.png" style="border-width: medium; border-style: none;" width="18"/></a><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"> in the upper-right corner of the user interface<br/></span></li><li><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><span>Navigate to </span><strong>Self-Service &gt; Homepage</strong><span> in the application navigator<br/><br/></span></span></li></ul></li><li><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">Click the words <strong>Add Content</strong> (in Dublin and earlier releases) or the plus symbol.<br/><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-3422-13521/customize_homepage3.png"><img  alt="customize_homepage3.png" class="jive-image image-3" height="162" src="0fb2bc02db589304b322f4621f961935.iix" style="width: 584px; height: 162.012903225806px;" width="585"/></a></span></li><li><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">In the <strong>Add content</strong> popup, click items in the left column. <br/><br/>Items include gadgets, live feed, world clocks, and diagnostics. Different options for the selected item appear in the next column to the right. The far right column provides a third level of options if available. In the example, <strong>Gauges</strong> was selected in the left column, <strong>Incident</strong> in the middle column, and <strong>All Incidents By Location</strong> in the right column. <br/><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-3422-13522/customize_homepage4.png"><img  alt="customize_homepage4.png" class="image-0 jive-image" height="408" src="e4e1dccedb949704ed6af3231f96194a.iix" style="height: 407.683229813665px; width: 459px;" width="459"/></a><br/><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">A preview of the selected item appears just below the columns.</span><br/><br/></span></li><li><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">To add an item to your homepage, click any of the <strong>Add here</strong> boxes.<br/><br/>The boxes show approximately where the item will be located on the page: top, bottom, left, or right. The boxes are also called <em>dropzones</em>.<br/></span></li></ol><p style="text-align: -webkit-auto;"><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;"><br/></span></p><h3><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">Here are some tips and tricks for customizing your homepage:<br/><br/></span></h3><ul><li><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">Gauges are great. They are visual and give you current information is an easy to use format. They are one of the coolest and most useful items you can add to your homepage. There is one important caveat to keep in mind, though. Every gauge is a report that must run each time your homepage refreshes. If you add a lot of gauges to your homepage and the page loads slowly, try removing a few of those fabulous gauges</span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;">. For more information about gauges, see <span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #3366ff; text-decoration: underline;">Adding Existing Gauges to a Homepage</span></span> in the product documentation.</span></li></ul><p style="padding-left: 30px;"><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-3422-13520/customize_homepage2.png"><img  alt="customize_homepage2.png" class="jive-image image-2" height="340" src="2c595582db98130468c1fb651f961923.iix" style="height: 339.553418803419px; width: 433px;" width="433"/></a><br/></span></p><ul><li><span style="text-align: -webkit-auto; color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;">Click <strong>Refresh</strong> to reload your homepage at any time. You can also specify a time interval to automatically refresh. This option is <strong>Off</strong> by default. To change the refresh setting, click the menu to the right of the <strong>Refresh</strong> option and select a time such as 5 or 30 minutes.<br/><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-3422-13519/customize_homepage1.png"><img  alt="customize_homepage1.png" class="image-1 jive-image" height="82" src="7183b379dbdc93041dcaf3231f9619e6.iix" style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto; height: 82.0459770114943px; width: 415px;" width="415"/></a></span></li><li><span style="text-align: -webkit-auto; color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;">You can change the layout of your homepage for greater control over where homepage items are placed. <span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">Open your homepage and in the </span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><strong>Switch to page</strong></span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"> list on the right side of the header, select </span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><strong>Change layout</strong></span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">. Click on different options and view a sample at the bottom of the screen. In the example, the <strong>3 columns with 2 wide columns and a narrow right column</strong> was selected and the preview shows the different dropzones for that option.<br/><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-3422-13523/customize_homepage5.png"><img  alt="customize_homepage5.png" class="image-0 jive-image" height="205" src="5d00ff3ddb589b048c8ef4621f9619b3.iix" style="height: 204.773553719008px; width: 534px;" width="535"/></a><br/></span></span></li><li><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">An alternative to customizing your homepage is creating a new personal homepage only you can see. Open your homepage and in the </span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><strong>Switch to page</strong></span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"> list on the right side of the header, select </span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"><strong>New page</strong></span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">. </span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">Administrators can create a homepage for users with a specific or create a global homepage accessible to all users. </span><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;">For more information, see </span><a class="jive-link-external-small" href="http://wiki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=Creating_New_Homepages" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #3366ff; text-decoration: underline;">Creating Homepages</span></span></a><span style="color: #333333; font-family: Omnes-pro, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif; text-align: -webkit-auto;"> in the product documentation.</span></li></ul><p style="text-align: -webkit-auto;"><span style="color: #333333;
