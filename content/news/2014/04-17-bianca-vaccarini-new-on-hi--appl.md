<p>The <a title="k-external-small" class="jive-link-external-small" href="https://hi.service-now.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">HI</a> user interface has changed! With our latest update, we have added a few new things to HI that will make navigating the HI portal easier and more efficient. A few icons have received a facelift, some fields have moved and new features have been added.</p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><h2>Type filter text in the Application Navigator</h2><p>The usual font size editor and applications buttons have been replaced with a type filter text field. You can now mark items as favorites, using the star in the subheading, and filter your favorites.</p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-2997-9158/Screen Shot 2014-04-10 at 4.50.03 PM (2).png"><img  alt="Screen Shot 2014-04-10 at 4.50.03 PM (2).png" class="image-0 jive-image" height="298" src="dbba148adb5c17041dcaf3231f9619bd.iix" style="height: 298px; width: 299.257px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="299"/></a></p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><h2>Toggle Navigator, List and Form View, Live Feed and more on The EDGE</h2><p>The EDGE has some new functions including Toggle Navigator, List and Form View, Tagged Documents, All Bookmarks and Live Feed. The toggle navigator allows you to switch to difference views in HI. The List and Form View button will allow you to see a quick view of your lists and forms more efficiently. The Tagged Documents button will reveal difference records that you have tagged. You can now access your all of your bookmarks using the All Bookmarks button, which now have new icons and colors. The Live Feed feature will allow you to access a live stream of information for all records displayed in a list.</p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-2997-9159/Screen Shot 2014-04-09 at 2.23.38 PM.png"><img  alt="Screen Shot 2014-04-09 at 2.23.38 PM.png" class="image-1 jive-image" height="431" src="8d724c46db1c5704ed6af3231f961997.iix" style="height: auto; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="310"/></a></p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><h2>Tagging documents</h2><p>You can create tags on forms, use them to categorize documents, and view them all in one place.</p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-2997-9161/Screen Shot 2014-04-10 at 3.56.35 PM (1).png"><img  alt="Screen Shot 2014-04-10 at 3.56.35 PM (1).png" class="jive-image image-3" height="840" src="3ee0c5c2db1897041dcaf3231f961913.iix" style="height: 326px; padding: 0px;">  </p><h2>Search list columns</h2><p>You can now search within a list in a column using the search icon at the top of each column.</p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-2997-9162/Screen Shot 2014-04-10 at 4.04.20 PM.png"><img  alt="Screen Shot 2014-04-10 at 4.04.20 PM.png" class="jive-image image-5" height="571" src="83f06c82db50d704ed6af3231f9619c4.iix" style="height: 222px; No matter how far down you scroll in the list, the column heading will remain visible at the top of the column.</p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-2997-9163/column headers.png"><img  alt="column headers.png" class="image-1 jive-image" height="799" src="8e8084c6db58d3049c9ffb651f9619eb.iix" style="height: 703px; width: 620px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="705"/></a></p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><h2>Record titles</h2><p>You will never forget the incident record with the new record titles in the updated UI. Record numbers can be found in the form header.</p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><p><a _jive_internal="true" href="/servlet/JiveServlet/showImage/38-2997-9164/Screen Shot 2014-04-10 at 4.37.49 PM.png"><img  alt="Screen Shot 2014-04-10 at 4.37.49 PM.png" class="jive-image image-6" height="703" src="5df36d4adb1cd304b322f4621f9619d5.iix" style="height: 273px; width: 620px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="1600"/></a></p><p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;">  </p><h2>Updated icons and buttons</h2><p>The new look has some new buttons and updated icons.
