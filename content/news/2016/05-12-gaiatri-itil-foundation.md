ITIL stands for IT Infrastructure Library. </span></p><p></p><p style="font-family: arial, sans-serif; color: #666666;"><span style="font-style: inherit; font-size: 12pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; color: #3d3d3d;">ITIL focuses on aligning IT services with business needs. It provides guidance to individuals and organizations on using IT to facilitate business change, transformation and growth. ITIL provides a framework that companies can adapt to meet their own needs.</span></p><p></p><p style="margin-bottom: 30px; font-size: 15px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; color: #373737;"><span style="font-style: inherit; font-size: 12pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">ITIL consists of 3 lifecycle phases.
