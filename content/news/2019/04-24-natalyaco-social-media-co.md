<p>There is something for everyone at Knowledge 2019! Participate in one of our social media contests and you could leave Vegas with an Oculus virtual reality headset, Sonos Beam Smart TV Soundbar with Alexa, or a Lego Mindstorm remote controlled robot kit.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Ready to get started? <strong>Once you follow &#64;Know365 on Twitter</strong>, here&#39;s everything else you need to know:</p>
<p>1. We&#39;ll be posting photos from &#64;Know365 throughout the week and want to see you flex your creative muscles! Submit your cleverest captions to the #Know19Caption contest and you could win a Swag Bag.<br />2. Participate in the #Know19Games Scavenger Hunt. Up to 3 attendees who tweet photos with the most items on our Scavenger Hunt list will win either an Oculus VR headset, Lego Mindstorm robot kit or Sonos speakers! (See below for full Scavenger Hunt list)</p>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;">#Know19Games Scavenger Hunt</span><br />Post your photos and thoughts to Twitter with as many items on this list as you can using the hashtag #Know19Games for a chance to win! Must be completed by 12:00pm PST on Thursday, May 9.</p>
<p>• The WORK LOVE WORK wall in the Workflow Lounge<br />• The Hackathon or CreatorCon® Challenge<br />• Your photo from the “Take work to the next level” photo booth<br />• A friend you made at Knowledge®<br />• Your favorite keynote or session (and tell us why it was your favorite)</p>
