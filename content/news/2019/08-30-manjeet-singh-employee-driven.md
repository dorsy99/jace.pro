<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Digital transformation is at the tip of everyone’s lips. Yet many organizations and the tools they use are siloed, making it difficult to properly drive transformation or manage ideas from concept to continual improvement and to measurable business value.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Companies that support an ideation and <a href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/newyork-it-service-management/page/product/continual-improvement-management/concept/cim-landing-page.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">continuous improvement</a> culture encourage all of their employees to participate in the submission of ideas. Who knows better about ways to transform than the employees who work there? Fostering this culture encourages all levels of the organization to support continual improvement and transformation.</span></p>
<p><br /><span style="font-size: 14pt;">The ServiceNow <a href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/newyork-it-business-management/page/product/innovation-management/concept/innovation-management.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">Idea Management app</a> enables you to gather and evaluate ideas efficiently, and to quickly identify the ideas with the greatest potential for improvement. The <a href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/newyork-it-business-management/page/product/innovation-management/concept/idea-portal.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">Idea Portal </a>available as part of Idea Management app provides Service Portal based interface to the users to view, submit, vote, and subscribe to ideas. This makes it really easy for all employees to submit ideas through already familiar user interface.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">As an Idea Manager, you can promote an idea to a continual improvement initiative to bring all improvement related work in to <a href="https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/newyork-it-service-management/page/product/continual-improvement-management/concept/cim-improvement-initiative-mgmt.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">improvement register</a>.</span></p>
