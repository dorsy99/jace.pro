<h4>What&#39;s new with development in New York?</h4>
<h5>All application development starts in the Guided App Creator </h5>
<p>Whether you start creating an app in Studio or in the My Company Applications list, the Guided App Creator launches to guide you through the steps to create the app, then you configure it and add functionality in Mobile Studio. </p>
<p>See a demo of creating a mobile app in our video below:</p>
<p><iframe id="video_tinymce" style="width: 100%; height: 480px;" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/oieGPMG0Fug"></iframe></p>
<h5>Creating a mobile app creates a list applet by default</h5>
<p>When you build a mobile app in the Guided App Creator, a list applet is created for you that you can configure, or delete if you don&#39;t need it. </p>
<p>In our video below, we show you how to configure a list applet:</p>
