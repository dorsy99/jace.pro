<h3>Upgrade faster, reduce build costs, and increase productivity</h3>
<p> </p>
<p>Date: <strong>February 21, 2019</strong></p>
<p>Time: 9:00 AM Pacific|12:00 PM Eastern</p>
<p>............................................................................................</p>
<p>To unlock the full potential of your ServiceNow solution, upgrading consistently is critical. Join this webinar to learn the specific benefits a recent Forrester Consulting study uncovered.</p>
<p>Anish Shah, Manager and Principal, TEI Practice at Forrester will walk you through the results of a recent study that quantified the benefits of upgrading consistently—and Christopher Whitesell, Senior Solutions Engineer at Fairview Health, will discuss the specific benefits that his organization has seen.</p>
<p>Find out how upgrading faster helps organizations:</p>
<ul><li>Upgrade and maintain their ServiceNow instances faster and easier</li><li>Reduce the cost to build out new functionality</li><li>Increase productivity by providing faster access to new time-saving features</li></ul>
