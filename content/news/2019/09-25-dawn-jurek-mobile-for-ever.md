<p>Wouldn&#39;t it be great if all your employees everywhere could leverage the power of the Now Platform on a mobile device to get work done anywhere?</p>
<p>The Now Mobile app offered in the New York release empowers all employees to manage their work lives with features for:</p>
<ul><li>Requesting help</li><li>Viewing items that belong to them and reporting any issues</li><li>Browsing services</li><li>Tracking the status of a request</li><li>Viewing knowledge bases</li><li>Viewing and completing tasks</li><li>...and more!</li></ul>
<p>In this installment of our <a title="NOWSupport best practices series" href="https://community.servicenow.com/community?id&#61;community_blog&amp;sys_id&#61;6e7d6269dbd0dbc01dcaf3231f9619c0" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">NOWSupport best practices series</a>, we answer your questions and show you how to get started with the Now Mobile app, how admins can configure the app, and where to learn more via videos, product documentation, and a demo you can try out for free!</p>
<p>________________________________________________</p>
<h3>How many mobile apps does ServiceNow offer?</h3>
<p>With the Madrid release, ServiceNow offered the first native mobile app: Mobile Agent.</p>
<p>In the New York release, we expanded our offerings to include two new apps: Now Mobile, and Mobile Onboarding. Check out our short overview video below to see the features of each app.</p>
