<p><p><img src="http://i.imgur.com/NSg8z.jpg" alt="Groups" width="150px" align='right' /><br />Assignment Routing (Rules), can speed up the Incident/Task Resolution process greatly. Getting that task to the right group on a timely basis is a key to the success of your ITSM application.<br /><br />In this article, I'll show a customization to allow a default assignment group for a logged in user. This is helpful for setting an initial assignment group when the user creates a new task.<br /><!--break--><br />Also be aware that there is also System Policy for Incidents that companies often use,
