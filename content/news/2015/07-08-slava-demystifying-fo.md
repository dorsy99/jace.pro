<p style="text-align: justify;">There are a few mysterious things in ServiceNow that are often believed to be out of administrators' reach. These are various pieces of the core functionality that simply work the way they do. Tabbed forms are definitely one of them. In this post, I will show you how to deal with form tabs and teach you some tricks that you are not going to find in the Wiki.</p><p style="text-align: justify;"></p><p style="text-align: justify;">Toggling tabs on/off is very well explained in <a title="w.servicenowguru.com/scripting/client-scripts-scripting/toggle-tabs-client-scripting/" href="http://www.servicenowguru.com/scripting/client-scripts-scripting/toggle-tabs-client-scripting/">this post</a> by Mark Stanger. There are, however, a few other things you may want to do with tabbed forms. To manage related lists in a similar manner, use <strong>g_tabs2List</strong> object instead.</p><p style="text-align: justify;"></p><p style="text-align: justify;">To give you an idea of how some of these methods can be used in a real life scenario, here is a script that enables switching between form sections using <strong>Alt+PageUp</strong> and <strong>Alt+PageDown</strong> combinations of keys:</p><p style="text-align: justify;"></p><pre __default_attr="javascript" __jive_macro_name="code" class="jive_text_macro jive_macro_code _jivemacro_uid_14362893701967580" jivemacro_uid="_14362893701967580" modifiedtitle="true">
<p style="text-align: justify;">addLoadEvent(function() {</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">     $$('body').each(function (elm) {</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">           Event.observe(elm, 'keyup', function(event) {</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">                 if (g_tabs2Sections.activated &amp;&amp; event.altKey) {</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">                       var btnCode = event.keyCode ? event.keyCode : event.charCode;</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">                       var tabsNumber = g_tabs2Sections.tabs.length;</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">                       var currentTab = g_tabs2Sections.activeTab * 1;</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">                       var nextTab = 0;</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">                       if (btnCode == 33)</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">                             nextTab = currentTab - 1;</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">                       if (btnCode == 34)</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">                             nextTab = currentTab + 1;</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">                       if (nextTab &gt; -1 &amp;&amp; nextTab &lt; tabsNumber)</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">                             g_tabs2Sections.setActive(nextTab);</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">                 }</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">           });</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">     });</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">});</p>
