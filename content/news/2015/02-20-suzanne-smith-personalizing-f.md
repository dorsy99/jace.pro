Â  </em></span></p><p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 13.63636302948px; line-height: 1.5em;"><em>- Mick</em></span><span style="font-size: 13.63636302948px; line-height: 1.5em;"><em> Ebeling</em></span></p><p></p><p><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;">With the Fuji release, you can now personalize forms just for yourself. Yes, you can <a title="ki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=Personalizing_Forms" href="http://wiki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=Personalizing_Forms">personalize the fields that appear on a form view</a> and no one else is affected by your changes. </span></p><p><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;"><br/></span></p><p><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;">Personalizing a form is different than <a title="ki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=Configuring_Forms" href="http://wiki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=Configuring_Forms">configuring a form</a>. When you personalize the form, you choose fields from the Personalize Form list to change how the form appears to <strong>you</strong>. Users with the itil role can personalize forms. Configuring a form changes the way the form looks for <em><strong>all</strong></em><strong> users</strong>. Administrators and users with the personalize_form role can configure forms. </span></p><p><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;"><br/></span></p><h2>How to personalize a form:</h2><p></p><ol><li>Open a record. For example, an incident, problem, or change record.</li><li>Click the gear icon <img   alt="personalize_form1.jpg" class="image-0 jive-image jiveImage" height="17" src="cbd5f7b1db1cdf04e9737a9e0f96192f.iix" width="17"/>.</li><li>Do one of the following:<ul><li><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;">Clear the check box next to a field in the <strong>Personalize Form</strong> list (the list shows only the fields that the form is configured to display)</span></li><li><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;">Click the hide icon </span><img   alt="personalize_form2.jpg" class="image-1 jive-image jiveImage" src="8bb8518edb58130468c1fb651f961970.iix" style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em; height: auto;"/><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;"> next to a field<br/><br/>For example, to hide the <strong>Subcategory</strong> field, click the hide icon next to the <strong>Subcategory</strong> field or clear the <strong>Subcategory</strong> check box in the <strong>Personalize Form</strong> list.<br/><img   alt="personalize_form3.jpg" class="jive-image image-2" src="60f5b80adb9c5f048c8ef4621f9619b7.iix" style="height: 387px; width: 620px;"/></span><br/>Note that any check boxes in the Personalize Form list that are grey and selected cannot be hidden. These fields are either required or are hidden by a UI policy or client script. For example, in the list below the <strong>Close code</strong> and <strong>Close notes</strong> fields cannot be hidden.<br/><img   alt="personalize_form4.jpg" class="jive-image image-3" src="0d9818c2db989fc068c1fb651f96195c.iix" style="height: auto;"/></li></ul></li><li>To save your changes, click anywhere outside the Personalize Form list.</li></ol><p></p><h3>Here are a few tips when personalizing forms:</h3><ul><li>After personalizing a form, your form changes are retained for the future.</li><li>The first time you personalize a form, all available fields appear in the Personalize Form list. If you hide any fields using the Personalize Form list, those fields will no longer appear in the list. Reset the form to see all fields in the list again.</li><li>You can reset the form to the default settings at any time by clicking the gear icon and then clicking <strong>Reset.</strong></li></ul><p></p><p><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;">ServiceNow offers additional resources for personalizing your forms in Fuji:</span></p><p><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;"><a href="http://wiki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=View_Management" title="http://wiki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=View_Management">View Management</a></span></p><p><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;"><a href="http://wiki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=Administering_Form_Personalization" title="http://wiki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=Administering_Form_Personalization">Administering Form Personalization</a></span></p><p><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;"><a title="Dot-walking and personalizing forms" __default_attr="3802" __jive_macro_name="blogpost" class="jive_macro jive_macro_blogpost" data-orig-content="Dot-walking and personalizing forms" href="/community?id=community_blog&sys_id=a44ea2addbd0dbc01dcaf3231f9619b4">Dot-walking and personalizing forms</a></span></p>
