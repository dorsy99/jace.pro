<p style="background: white;"><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: Arial; color: #485563;">In <a title="ki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=Navigation_and_the_User_Interface#gsc.tab=0" href="http://wiki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=Navigation_and_the_User_Interface#gsc.tab=0">UI15</a>, when viewing a request item that has the list collector variable on the form, you are prompted that you have made a change when leaving/reloading the page. </span><span style="color: #485563; font-family: Arial; font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em; text-indent: 0.5in;">If a list collector variable is on a form and the changeFlag is set to 'true,' the system will interpret this as something on the form being changed. This results in a <a title="i.service-now.com/kb_view.do?sysparm_article=KB0549734" href="https://hi.service-now.com/kb_view.do?sysparm_article=KB0549734">"Confirm Reload" dialog box to pop up</a> with two options: reload the page, or don't reload and continuing will discard the changes. </span></p><p style="background: white;"><span style="color: #485563; font-family: Arial; font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em; text-indent: 0.5in;"><br/></span></p><p style="text-align: center; background: white;"><span style="line-height: 1.5em; text-indent: 48px; color: #485563; font-size: 10pt; font-family: Arial;"><em>Note: This can also occur on other forms with the variable editor.</em></span></p><p></p><p style="margin-bottom: 6.75pt; background: white;"><img   alt="Blog pic 5.jpg" class="image-0 jive-image" src="00fe50c6db9c9fc068c1fb651f96191d.iix" style="height: auto; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"/></p><p>To workaround the list collector variable triggering the "Confirm Reload" dialog box, c<span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;">reate an </span><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;"><strong>onLoad</strong></span><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;"> </span><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;"><strong>Client Script</strong></span><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;"> on </span><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;"><strong>Request Item (sc_req_item)</strong></span><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;"> or any other form that has a variable editor. </span></p><p><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;"><br/></span></p><p><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;">Use this code:</span></p><pre __default_attr="plain" __jive_macro_name="code" class="jive_text_macro jive_macro_code _jivemacro_uid_14419105995034436" jivemacro_uid="_14419105995034436">
