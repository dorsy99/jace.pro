<p>The homepage for a Service Catalog provides a primary spot for ordering catalog items. Administrators can <a title="ki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=Managing_the_Homepage_for_a_Catalog#Customizing_the_Homepage_for_a_Catalog" href="http://wiki.servicenow.com/index.php?title=Managing_the_Homepage_for_a_Catalog#Customizing_the_Homepage_for_a_Catalog">customize the design</a> and create catalog category widgets, such as the Shopping Cart or Top Requests, to display on their page.</p><p></p><h2>How to customize the Service Catalog homepage:</h2><ol><li>Navigate to <strong>Service Catalog &gt; Catalog</strong>.</li><li>Click <strong>+</strong> symbol at the top of the page to add a category.</li><li>Select a category in the middle panel of the pop-up screen.</li><li>Click <strong>Add here</strong> where the category should appear on the homepage.<br/><br/></li></ol><p><img   alt="Widgets_Arrows.jpg" class="image-0 jive-image" src="67819902db1813043eb27a9e0f96196b.iix" style="height: 341px; width: 620px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; border: #BDBDBD 4px solid; border-radius: 15px;"/></p><p><span style="font-family: Helvetica;">If you've created your <a title="i.service-now.com/kb_view.do?sysparm_article=KB0551326" href="https://hi.service-now.com/kb_view.do?sysparm_article=KB0551326">shopping cart widget but the title is not being displayed properly</a> in Fuji, you may need to do a manual configuration to the widget in order to view this. The title is initially controlled by cart layouts, with the system preference for the Shopping Cart widget set to <strong>false</strong> (</span><span style="font-family: courier new,courier;">glide.sc.use_cart</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;"><span style="font-family: courier new,courier;">_layouts</span>). <span><span style="font-size: 10pt;">However,</span> </span></span>when you create the Shopping cart widget, you may find that your title disappears:</p><p><span style="font-family: Calibri;"><br/></span></p><p><span style="font-family: Calibri;"><img   alt="No_title_shopping_cart.jpg" class="jive-image image-6" src="a220b0cedb58dfc068c1fb651f96194b.iix" style="height: 225px; width: 620px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; border: #BDBDBD 4px solid; border-radius: 15px;"/></span><span style="font-family: Calibri;"><br/></span></p><p>To fix this, you'll need to use the <strong>Editing Widget</strong> icon to view the title for this widget.</p><p></p><h2>How to display the Shopping Cart widget title:</h2><p></p><ol><li>Navigate to <strong>Service Catalog &gt; Catalog</strong>.</li><li>Hover over the <strong>Shopping Cart</strong> widget and click the <strong>Editing Widget </strong>gear icon:<br/><br/><img   alt="WidgetGear.jpg" class="jive-image image-2" src="9afdecc2db585f048c8ef4621f961935.iix" style="height: 219px; width: 620px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; border: #BDBDBD 4px solid; border-radius: 15px;"/></li><li>Check <strong>Title</strong>.</li><li>Click <strong>Done</strong>.</li></ol><p><img   alt="Shopping Cart widget.jpg" class="jive-image image-3" src="48a2540adbd49704ed6af3231f96197d.iix" style="height: 312px; width: 620px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; border: #BDBDBD 4px solid; border-radius: 15px;"/></p><p>This should ensure that your title stays put on your Catalog page:</p><p></p><p><img   alt="shopping_cart_viewable.jpg" class="jive-image image-4" src="2bfb2fbddb14df04e9737a9e0f9619be.iix" style="height: 398px; width: 620px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; border: #BDBDBD 4px solid; border-radius: 15px;"/></p><p></p><p>Can't shop 'til you drop because of Service Catalog issues?
