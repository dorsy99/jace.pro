<p style="text-align: center;"><em>"I don't think you'll ever have a perfect world because we humans are prone to error, and so we're always in search of an upgrade."</em></p><p style="text-align: center;">-Henry Rollins</p><p class="p1"><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em; text-align: left;"><br/></span></p><p class="p1"><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em; text-align: left;">The Instance Upgrade Management dashboard is a great feature in the new <a _jive_internal="true" href="/community/service-automation-platform/user-interface/blog/2015/03/27/self-service-delivered-now"><span style="color: #0000ff;">Customer Self-Service Portal</span></a>. The dashboard serves as a hub for controlling upgrades on all of your instances. View the current version for all of your instances, <a title="" _jive_internal="true" href="/community/product-launch/instance-upgrade/blog/2015/07/20/simplified-hi-entitlement-process">request a version entitlement</a>, schedule an upgrade, modify a scheduled upgrade, or cancel an upgrade from one handy location. ServiceNow partners will find the dashboard useful because they can view their own instances and the instances of the companies they manage.</span></p><p></p><h3>Requesting an upgrade</h3><p></p><p><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;">All customers can upgrade their instances to a newer version or to the latest version of ServiceNow.</span></p><ol><li>Open HI at <a class="jive-link-external-small" href="https://hi.service-now.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><span style="color: #0000ff;">hi.service-now.com</span></a><span style="color: #0000ff;">.</span></li><li>Click <strong>Manage Upgrades</strong>.</li><li><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;">In the </span><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;"><strong>Instance</strong></span><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;"> column, locate the instance that you want to upgrade.</span></li><li><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;">In the <strong>Upgrade Available</strong> column, select a version.<br/><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;"><br/>The list includes versions for which you have an entitlement and any early access versions to which you have been invited. The newest available and recommended upgrade is shown by default. Other upgrade options are listed in decreasing order of recommendation. If an instance is already running the newest version, then </span><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;"><strong>Up To Date</strong></span><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;"> appears and no upgrade options are available.<br/><br/><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;">If the upgrade version you want is not listed in the </span><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;"><strong>Upgrade Available</strong></span><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;"> column for a specific instance, click <strong>Need a version not listed</strong>.</span><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;"> Answer two questions on the version entitlement request form and submit the request. An acknowledgement email is sent right away and a ServiceNow entitlement manager sends an approval or rejection email message within two days. A reason is provided for all rejected requests. <em>See <a title="Simplifying the HI Entitlement Process" __default_attr="4402" __jive_macro_name="blogpost" class="jive_macro jive_macro_blogpost" data-orig-content="Simplifying the HI Entitlement Process" href="/community?id=community_blog&sys_id=430e2e2ddbd0dbc01dcaf3231f961980">Simplifying the HI Entitlement Process</a> for changes made to the entitlement process as of 7/15/2015.</em><br/><br/></span><img   alt="upgrade_dashboard.jpg" class="image-0 jive-image" src="5599bff5db501304b322f4621f9619d7.iix" style="height: 273px; width: 620px;"/><br/></span><br/></span></li><li><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;">Click </span><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;"><strong>Schedule</strong></span><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;"> in the </span><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;"><strong>Actions</strong></span><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;"> column.</span></li><li><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;">Specify a date and time for the upgrade.</span></li><li><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;">Click <strong>Schedule</strong>.<br/>The version, upgrade date, and upgrade time appear in the <strong>Status</strong> column for the instance being upgraded.<br/><img   alt="upgrade_dashboard2.jpg" class="image-0 jive-image" src="04566586dbd0df048c8ef4621f9619af.iix" style="height: 172px; width: 620px;"/><br/></span></li></ol><p><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;"><br/></span></p><h3>Changing the date and time of a scheduled upgrade</h3><p></p><p><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;">You can modify the date and time for an upgrade. Note the following:</span></p><ul><li><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;">If the change request is for an auto-upgrade, modify the upgrade date and time in the change request form.</span></li><li><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;">If the change request is for an upgrade you requested, follow the instructions in the next section to cancel the change request. Then, follow the instructions in the previous section to request a new upgrade specifying a new date and time<br/><br/></span></li></ul><ol><li>Open HI at <a class="jive-link-external-small" href="https://hi.service-now.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><span style="color: #0000ff;">hi.service-now.com</span></a>.</li><li>Click <strong>Manage Upgrades</strong>.</li><li><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;">Find the instance with the scheduled upgrade that you want to modify and click </span><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;"><strong>Modify CHG</strong></span><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;"> in the <strong>Actions</strong> column.<img   alt="upgrade_dashboard3.jpg" class="image-1 jive-image" src="2cb101c6dbd09f048c8ef4621f9619f2.iix" style="height: 194px; width: 620px;"/></span><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;"><br/></span></li><li><span style="color: #485563; font-family: arial;">Change the date and time.<br/></span></li></ol><p></p><h3>Canceling an upgrade change request</h3><p><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;"><br/></span></p><p><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 1.5em;">Use this procedure to cancel an auto-upgrade or an upgrade you scheduled. If you cancel an auto-upgrade you are removed from the current auto-upgrade program.</span></p><ol><li>Open HI at <a class="jive-link-external-small" href="https://hi.service-now.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><span style="color: #0000ff;">hi.service-now.com</span></a>.</li><li>Click <strong>Manage Upgrades</strong>.</li><li><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Arial, Verdana, Helvetica, sans-serif;">Find the scheduled upgrade you want to cancel and click </span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Arial, Verdana, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><strong>Modify CHG</strong></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Arial, Verdana, Helvetica, sans-serif;"> in the </span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Arial, Verdana, Helvetica, sans-serif;"><strong>Actions</strong></span><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Arial, Verdana, Helvetica, sans-serif;"> column.</span></li><li><span style="color: #000000; font-family: Arial, Verdana, Helvetica, sans-serif;">On the change request form, click <strong>Cancel Upgrade</strong>. </span></li></ol><p><span style="font-size: 10pt;
